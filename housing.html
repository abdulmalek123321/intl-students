🏠 Student Housing Options in Malaysia

Choose between on‑campus or off‑campus housing. Below are detailed options for UTHM, UM, and UTM.

🏫 On-Campus Accommodation

UTM (Universiti Teknologi Malaysia)

Offers 10 residential colleges hosting up to 14,774 students plus 301 housing units for families. Furnished flats or townhouses shared by 2–3 students with basic amenities. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

UM (University of Malaya)

Offers 11 in‑campus residential colleges (plus 2 off‑campus). Basic hostels, some include cafeterias and common areas. Secure and managed by university. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

UTHM (Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia)

University-run dormitories available on campus. Managed via Student Housing and Transportation Centre. Functional and reliable. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

🏠 Off-Campus Accommodation

UTM – Johor Bahru Area

Areas like Taman Universiti, Kangkar Pulai offer rooms for RM 350‑600/month or flats RM 500‑800. Often furnished, near shops and transport. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

UM – Kuala Lumpur Area

Numerous shared apartments, PBSA, or studios available. Most rent between RM 700–1300/month. Examples: Goodwood Residence, Tiara Imperio. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

UTHM – Batu Pahat Area

Local hostels, rental houses, and apartments near campus. Monthly rates vary; expect RM 300–700 depending on facilities and distance.
