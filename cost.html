💰 Cost of Living & Study in Malaysia

🏡 Accommodation

  • On-campus housing: RM 300 - RM 600 / month
  • Off-campus housing: RM 500 - RM 1000 / month (depends on location and facilities)

🍽️ Food & Dining

  • Local meals: RM 5 - RM 10 per meal
  • Monthly food budget: RM 300 - RM 600

🚍 Transportation

  • University buses (free within campus)
  • Public transport: RM 50 - RM 100 / month

📶 Internet & Mobile

  • Internet: RM 100 / month (home or campus Wi-Fi)
  • Mobile phone & data plan: RM 30 - RM 60 / month

🎓 Tuition Fees (Approximate)

  • UTM / UTHM: RM 6,000 - RM 9,000 per year (varies by program)
  • English language courses: RM 1,500 - RM 3,000 per course

💡 Money Saving Tips

  • Share accommodation with roommates
  • Cook your own meals
  • Use student discount cards wherever possible

📊 Average Monthly Expenses

Estimated total: RM 1000 - RM 1800 per month
