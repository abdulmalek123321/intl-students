Student Visa Application Guide

📝 Step 1: Receive an Offer Letter

Apply and get an official offer letter from a recognized Malaysian university.

📄 Step 2: Submit Visa Application

Submit required documents (passport, photo, offer letter, medical checkup) to EMGS via your university.

⌛ Step 3: Wait for Visa Approval

Processing usually takes 2–6 weeks. Track status via EMGS website.

✈️ Step 4: Travel to Malaysia

Once approved, receive Visa Approval Letter (VAL) and book your ticket to Malaysia.

🏫 Step 5: Student Pass Endorsement

Upon arrival, submit your passport to your university for final student pass endorsement.
