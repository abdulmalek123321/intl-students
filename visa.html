🎓 Visa Information for International Students

Welcome to UTHM! As an international student, you'll need to obtain a valid Student Pass (visa) to stay and study in Malaysia. Here's what you need to know:

📌 Required Documents

  • Offer Letter from UTHM
  • Passport copy (all pages)
  • Medical Check-up Report
  • Passport-sized photo (white background)
  • Payment receipt for visa processing

🕒 Processing Time

Visa approval may take up to 4–6 weeks, so be sure to submit your documents early.

🛂 Upon Arrival in Malaysia

  • Submit your passport to the International Office
  • Your Student Pass sticker will be pasted inside your passport
  • Carry your I-KAD at all times

If you need help, visit the UTHM International Office or email io@uthm.edu.my
