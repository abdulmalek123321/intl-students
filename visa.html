🎓 Student Visa Process in Malaysia

Here's a clear and simple guide to help you get your student visa and start your journey in Malaysia with peace of mind.

✅ Step 1: Get Accepted

Apply to a Malaysian university and receive your official offer letter. This is the first requirement before applying for your visa.

📝 Step 2: Prepare Documents

Prepare a copy of your passport (all pages), medical check-up report, passport-sized photo, offer letter, and visa processing fee.

📤 Step 3: Submit via University

Submit your documents to your university. They will forward them to EMGS (Education Malaysia Global Services).

⏳ Step 4: Visa Processing

EMGS will process your visa in about 4–6 weeks. You can track your status online at the EMGS website.

✈️ Step 5: Travel to Malaysia

After receiving the Visa Approval Letter (VAL), you can book your flight and enter Malaysia with your documents ready.

🛂 Step 6: Final Student Pass

Upon arrival, submit your passport to your university’s international office to receive your student pass sticker.

ℹ️ For more details, visit: educationmalaysia.gov.my
