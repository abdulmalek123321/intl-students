Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM)

Location: Batu Pahat, Johor, Malaysia

Overview: UTHM is a leading public technical university focusing on engineering, technology, and applied sciences. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Programs:

  • Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, etc.)
  • Information Technology and Computer Science
  • Business and Management
  • Applied Sciences

Facilities: Modern labs, libraries, sports complex, student hostels, and research centers.

Official Website: https://www.uthm.edu.my
