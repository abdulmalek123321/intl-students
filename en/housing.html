🏡 Housing Guide for International Students in Malaysia

UTHM – Batu Pahat

  • In-campus: Kolej Kediaman Tun Dr. Ismail (KK TDI)
  • Nearby: Taman Universiti, Parit Raja, Taman Sri Sulong
  • Rent: RM 200 – RM 500/month (shared rooms)

UTM – Skudai

  • In-campus: Kolej 9 & 10, Kolej Tuanku Canselor
  • Nearby: Taman Universiti, Taman Sri Pulai Perdana
  • Rent: RM 300 – RM 600/month

🌐 Private Housing Platforms

  • Mudah.my – Malaysia's largest classifieds
  • iBilik.my – Find rooms across Malaysia
  • Facebook groups (search: “Student Housing Malaysia”)

💡 Tips for Choosing Housing

  • Always visit the room or request a video tour before paying.
  • Check distance to your campus and access to transport.
  • Prefer staying with other international students during your first months.
