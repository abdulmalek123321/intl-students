Student Visa Application Process in Malaysia

📝 Step 1: Receive University Offer Letter

Apply to a Malaysian university and obtain a formal offer letter.

📄 Step 2: Submit Visa Application via EMGS

Submit your documents (passport, medical report, offer letter, etc.) through your university to EMGS (Education Malaysia Global Services).

⌛ Step 3: Visa Approval Process

EMGS will process your visa. Track the status online. This takes around 2–6 weeks.

✈️ Step 4: Receive Visa Approval Letter (VAL)

Once approved, you’ll get the VAL. Use it to enter Malaysia through immigration.

🏫 Step 5: Endorse Student Pass in Malaysia

After arrival, submit your passport to your university for endorsement of the student pass sticker.

ℹ️ This process applies to all international students entering Malaysian universities through EMGS.
